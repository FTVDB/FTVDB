Fire TV and Kindle Update Database

Community-driven index of publicly accessible firmware update URLs for Fire TV and Kindle devices, plus Fire TV app update URLs. Use the tabs to browse versions, view details, or submit new links you have captured from your devices.

About

This is a fully automated indexing service. Submitted URLs appear in the database shortly and reflect update URLs captured from user-owned devices. FTVDB does not host any firmware or app files, and it does not promote firmware modifications, downgrades, or jailbreaking.
